# Classification Complete Checklist

> Quality gate: must pass before build handoff and `/evidence`

- [ ] Every friction point walked down the ladder in order; rejected rungs justified — or dispositioned Accept & Monitor with rationale and review date
- [ ] Safety tier (S/C/O) assigned to every item with named co-signer for S and C
- [ ] Tier S rule enforced: any disposition that removes, bypasses, or unattends a human safety-relevant decision point (Eliminate, Standardize-away, unattended Automate, or Delegate) has documented clinical governance approval naming the approving committee; Augment is the ceiling on PM authority alone
- [ ] Value figures use addressable friction only
- [ ] Register reconciles: sum of expected value capture does not exceed total addressable friction; shared friction allocated explicitly
- [ ] Eliminate/Simplify/Standardize items sequenced ahead of build work
- [ ] Every rung 4-6 survivor has a complete handoff pack (value, effort, dependencies, residual human role) on a Disposition Scorecard
- [ ] Every rung 4-6 survivor has a named baseline-freeze owner (Phase E cannot audit without a pre-go-live baseline)
- [ ] `docs/disposition/` artifacts exist and are complete
